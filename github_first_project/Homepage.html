Brittle Bones Preservations Inc.

Helping you find the beauty within the inevitable.

Welcome to Brittle Bones Preservations!

Here at Brittle Bones we transform the finite into the everlasting. Our mission is to honor life through the delicate art of bone taxidermy. We specialize in two deeply personal and meaningful areas:

✦ Pet Memorials: We believe every beloved pet deserves a beautiful and lasting tribute. Working only with ethically sourced and respectfully prepared remains, we create elegant, dignified memorial pieces that preserve the memory and form of your companion for years to come.

✦ Unique Art Pieces: Beyond memorials, we craft one-of-a-kind art installations—from intricate sculptures to statement decor—that celebrate the natural architecture and surprising beauty of the skeletal form.

At Brittle Bones Inc., our work is an act of deep respect for the cycle of life. Every bone is handled with care, cleaned meticulously, and thoughtfully arranged to create a piece of art that is both timeless and deeply personal. We don't just assemble bones; we give them a second life.

Where are we located?

Brittle Bones Preservations is a small, family-inspired business proudly rooted in the quiet, artistic community of Williamsburg, Tennessee. Surrounded by the raw, natural beauty of the foothills, our workshop is a space dedicated to honoring nature's final blueprint: the skeletal form.

While our roots are deeply set here in Tennessee, we are thrilled to serve a broader community. We accept online orders and provide secure, respectful delivery of our custom art and memorials throughout the entire United States.

A little more about us!

Brittle Bones Preservations was founded a decade ago by two lifelong friends, MJ and Jax, who met while both were in art school. The two shared a fascination with classic animal form studies, cultural approaches to memorialization, and Gothic art. They quickly recognized the potential to elevate respectful bone articulation from a scientific process into a meaningful, personalized art form.

Our core belief is that the end of a life is not an ending of beauty.

We are passionate about making respectful and intricate pieces of work to be cherished. some of the ways we do this is by drawing inspiration from historical anatomy museums, Memento Mori art, and global traditions that celebrate ancestors and animal spirits. Every piece is an act of respect. We work only with ethically-sourced materials, dedicating ourselves to the meticulous, dignified cleaning and preservation of remains. For pet memorials, we offer a service that transforms the sadness of loss into a permanent, stunning tribute—preserving the essence of a cherished companion's form.

Meet Our Team!

Meet the Artist: Mary J. (MJ) - Lead Articulator & Visionary

Mary J., affectionately known as MJ, is one of the foundational pillars of Brittle Bones Preservations. As our Lead Articulator and Visionary, she brings a profound depth of artistic insight and a meticulous hand to every piece we create. From a young age, MJ was captivated by the rich tapestry of global cultures, the intricate beauty of anatomy, and the powerful narratives woven into historical and Gothic art. This fascination evolved into a deep respect for the cycle of life and a unique appreciation for the inherent artistry within the skeletal form. She firmly believes that beauty doesn't cease at life's end, but rather transforms into a new, enduring aesthetic. Her talent lies in carefully cleaning, preserving, and articulating bones into stunning works of art, whether for a cherished pet memorial or an elaborate, standalone sculpture. She draws heavily from diverse cultural approaches to death, remembrance, and the spiritual significance of animal remains, infusing each creation with a sense of timeless reverence. With MJ at the helm of our articulation process, you can be assured that all materials are handled with the utmost ethical consideration and respect.

For MJ, every bone tells a story, and it is her passion to give that story a beautiful, lasting form. Her dedication to artistry and respect for the natural world are at the very heart of what makes Brittle Bones Preservations unique.

Meet the Artist: Jaxson - Creative Director & Memorial Designer

Jaxson is the dynamic Creative Director and heart behind the personalized memorial designs at Brittle Bones Preservations. One of our cherished founders, Jaxson brings an authentic, earthy-grungy aesthetic and a profound empathy to our work. Jaxson's journey into bone artistry was fueled by a deep appreciation for raw, natural beauty and a fascination with how different cultures approach remembrance and legacy. His personal style, marked by tattoos and piercings, reflects an appreciation for the unconventional and the artistry of transforming and adorning the self—a philosophy he extends to giving new life and beauty to natural remains. He works closely with clients seeking pet memorials, translating their love and memories into tangible, respectful, and uniquely designed tributes. Jaxson's eye for compelling compositions and his ability to blend natural elements with a modern, slightly rebellious edge define much of Brittle Bones' unique artistic output. He is often the first point of contact, guiding clients through the process with understanding and ensuring their vision for a memorial or art piece is beautifully realized.

Jaxson views each project as an opportunity to connect with the cycles of nature and life, finding powerful stories in what remains. His dedication ensures that every piece from Brittle Bones Preservations is not just an object, but a meaningful expression of art, memory, and respect.

Meet the Artist: Fynn - Preservationist & Naturalist

Fynn is the dedicated Preservationist and meticulous hands behind the initial transformation of materials at Brittle Bones Preservations. As a vital member of our founding trio, Fynn embodies the bridge between raw natural history and refined art. With a deep connection to the outdoors and a lifelong passion for natural sciences, Fynn approaches each piece with a naturalist's curiosity and a scientist's precision. Their distinctive style, subtly interwoven with earthy tones and nature motifs, reflects a reverence for the environment and the delicate balance of ecosystems. They ensure that all materials are acquired ethically and prepared with the utmost care, respect, and scientific rigor—a foundational step crucial to the integrity and longevity of every Brittle Bones piece. Fynn possesses an extensive knowledge of bone biology and preservation techniques, guaranteeing the highest quality and stability for both memorial pieces and artistic installations. Driven by their love for nature, Fynn continuously explores and implements environmentally conscious methods in our studio operations.

For Fynn, working with bones is an intimate act of stewardship, recognizing the beauty and wisdom inherent in every natural form. Their commitment to meticulous detail and ethical practice ensures that each creation from Brittle Bones Preservations is not only a work of art but a testament to profound respect for the natural world.
